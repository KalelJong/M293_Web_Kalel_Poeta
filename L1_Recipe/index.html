Carrot Cake recipe

Introduction

In this recipe you will learn to bake a simple traditional Brazilian carrot cake. For this recipe you will only need basic daily products and in less than 2 hours your cake will be ready to be enjoyed.

Dough ingredients

Amount Ingredient
300g Plain flour
400g Refined sugar
3 Medium peeled and sliced Carrots
1 teaspoon Baking powder
4 Large eggs
250ml Vegetable oil

Topping ingredients

Amount Ingredient
250ml Milk
1 tablespoon Butter
2 tablespoons Honey
100g Chocolate powder
