Carrot Cake recipe

Introduction

In this recipe you will learn to bake a simple traditional Brazilian carrot cake. For this recipe you will only need basic daily products and in less than 2 hours your cake will be ready to be enjoyed.

Dough ingredients

Name Amount
Plain flour 300g
Refined sugar 400g
Medium peeled and sliced Carrots 3
Baking powder 1 teaspoon
Large eggs 4
Vegetable oil/td> 250ml

Topping ingredients
