Carrot Cake recipe

Introduction

In this recipe you will learn to bake a simple traditional Brazilian carrot cake. For this recipe you will only need basic daily products and in less than 2 hours your cake will be ready to be enjoyed.

Dough ingredients

Amount Ingredient
300g Plain flour
400g Refined sugar
3 Medium peeled and sliced Carrots
1 teaspoon Baking powder
4 Large eggs
250ml Vegetable oil

Topping ingredients

Amount Ingredient
250ml Milk
1 tablespoon Butter
2 tablespoons Honey
100g Chocolate powder

Dough Preparation steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 CÂ°

  2. Peel and slice carrots

  3. Add in the carrots in the blender

  4. Pour mixture into a mixing bowl

  5. Add in the flour and baking powder

  6. Grease and flour 20 cm tin

  7. Pour batter into the tin and bake for 40 minutes

Topping Preparation steps

  1. In a saucepan add the milk, chocolate, honey and butter

  2. Over a medium flame keep stirring

  3. simmering away for 8-10 minutes until thickened

  4. Turn off the heat and allow to cool down fully

  5. pour the chocolate sauce topping over the cake
